4 AND 5 TOES

Norway Rat

Rattus norvegicus
To 18 in. (45 cm)
Large rodent has a gray to
brown coat and a scaly tail.
Hind tracks show 5 toes and
are 1.5-2.5 in. (4-6 cm) long.
Front tracks show 4 toes and
are about 1 in. (3 cm) long.

Woodrat

Neotoma spp.
To 16 in. (40 cm)
Gray-brown rodent has
whitish underparts and a
long, hairy tail. Hind tracks
are about 1 in. (3 cm) long
and have 5 toes. Front
tracks show 4 toes.

Deer Mouse

Peromyscus maniculatus
To 8 in. (20 cm)
Coat is brown to gray
above and white below.
Tail is hairy. Hind track is
about .7 in. (1.8 cm) long.
Front track shows 4 toes.

House Mouse

Mus musculus
To 8 in. (20 cm)
Distinguished by its grayish
coat and scaly tail. Hind
track is about .3 in. (.8 cm)
long and has 5 toes.

Hispid Cotton Rat

Sigmodon hispidus
To 15 in. (38 cm)
Small, short-eared rat has
coarse hair and is whitish
underneath. Hind track is
about 1 in. (3 cm) long
and has 5 toes.

Ord’s Kangaroo Rat

Dipodomys ordii
To 12 in. (30 cm)
Buff-colored rodent has
large hind feet and a long
tail. Hind tracks are about
1-2 in. (3-5 cm) long.
Front tracks are rounded.

Meadow Vole

Microtus pennsylvanicus
To 7 in. (18 cm)
Small, mouse-like creature has
long fur and a short tail.
Hind tracks are about
.5 in. (1.3 cm) long
and show 5 toes.

American Pika

Ochotona princeps
To 9 in. (23 cm)
Small, brownish rodent lives
on rocky mountain slopes.
Rounded tracks are about 1 in.
(3 cm) across. Front feet show
5 toes; hind feet show 4.
